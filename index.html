About Me

Halima Masud a multi-talented creative force from Edo State, Nigeria, blending the worlds of technology and traditiona craftsmanship. As a skilled front-end developer, i builds beautiful, responsive websites that provide seamless digital experiences. my passion for storytelling extends beyond code into the realm of poetry, where i weaves words with emotion and insight. With a deep appreciation for Digital Loom, i also designs and produces unique handmade wear for children. At just 23, am a remarkable example of a new generation of Nigerian creatives, who masterfully merge digital innovation with heartfelt, and tangible art.





These three disciplines, while different, are connected by a common thread: the act of creation. The precision of coding sharpens my eye for sewing, and the storytelling of poetry infuses my designs—both digital and physical—with heart and purpose.

Poem: " One Body, Diffrent Pattern"

Dem say Africa na just one place, Like say na only one expression dey our face. But if you look am well, you go understand The kind of wonder wey dey for this land. We be the cloth wey God himself design, One body, different pattern, wey dey shine.

Listen… you hear the rhythm of the drum? E dey call everybody say make dem come. The beat for wedding, beat wey sweet for street, The one wey make old woman move her feet. The pot of jollof wey dey cause friendly fight, The smell of suya burning in the night. Na different story grandmama dey tell, But all of them dey wish our children well.
